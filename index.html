Welcome! I'm Christine. I am a yoga teacher and software developer currently based in Omaha, Nebraska. I teach vinyasa, yin, and restorative classes. Check out my schedule to see when and where I'm teaching.

I received my Yoga Alliance RYT 200 certification at Lotus House of Yoga in Omaha, Nebraska in 2013. After teacher training I moved to Fargo, North Dakota where I was a software developer at Microsoft and taught yoga at Vayu Yoga, Family Wellness Yoga, and Mojo Fit Studios.

In May 2016, I quit my job at Microsoft to travel with my boyfriend, Pule. We had a great summer - read more about it here.

I love to learn new things and frequently attend trainings, festivals, conferences, and retreats. I most recently began a 300 hour teacher training at Lotus House of Yoga.

When I'm not practicing yoga or coding, you can catch me traveling, painting, working with yarn, climbing, doing something outside, cooking, or baking.

I would love to connect with you! Contact me on Instagram or through email.
